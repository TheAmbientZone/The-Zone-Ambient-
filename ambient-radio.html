How it works

Every channel is composed in real time on your phone — no streaming, nothing uploaded. A seeded engine builds a chord progression, evolving melodic motifs, tape-style wow & flutter, and a convolution reverb, then renders a seamless 200-second loop that crossfades into itself forever. Evolve quietly renders the next variation and slips it in at the loop point. Because each piece is fully determined by its seed + tone, a patch is just a short code — save it, or share it and a friend hears the exact same piece.

Hearing mode shapes the music for high-frequency hearing loss before it reaches your Bluetooth aids, so detail survives the codec. Clarity trims reverb and adds gentle high harmonics for intelligibility.
